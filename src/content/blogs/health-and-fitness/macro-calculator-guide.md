## Macronutrients (Macros) Kya Hote Hain?

Macronutrients un nutrients ko kaha jata hai jinki hamare sharir ko bahut badi (macro) matra mein zaroorat hoti hai. Jabki vitamins/minerals micronutrients hote hain (jo kam matra me zaruri hain), Macros directly body ko build karte hain aur urja dete hain.  

Hamare bhojan mein mukhyata teen tarah ke macronutrients paye jate hain:

### 1. Protein 
Muscles banane aur maintain karne wala mukhya hissa.
*   **Role (Kaam):** Workouts ke baad toote hue muscles tissues ki recovery, naye cells ka nirmaan (jaise baal, nakhun) aur immunity badhana.
*   **Calories:** 1 Gram Protein me 4 calories hoti hain.
*   **Sources (Strot):** Chicken breast, ande, paneer, tofu, daalein (lentils), soya chunks aur whey protein.

### 2. Carbohydrates (Carbs)
Body ki primary energy source.
*   **Role (Kaam):** Jab aap heavy weight uthate hain, daudte hain ya padhai karte hain, toh body brain aur muscles ko taakat dene ke liye naturally carbs ko sugar (glucose) me todti hai. Ye quick aur stable energy hain.
*   **Calories:** 1 Gram Carb me bhi 4 calories hoti hain.
*   **Sources (Strot):** Chawal (rice), aalu, daliya, oats, Roti aur phal.

### 3. Fats (Vasa)
Healthy aur long lasting energy storage.
*   **Role (Kaam):** Body ke harmone balance (jaise testosterone), joints ki lubrication, bad organs (brain function) ki safety, aur vitamins ko pachaane k lie fats zaruri hote hain.
*   **Calories:** 1 Gram Fat me 9 calories hoti hain (Isiliye inki thodi si quantity se bahut zada calories milti hain).
*   **Sources (Strot):** Desi ghee, olive oil, dry fruits (almonds/walnuts), peanut butter, aur avacado.

## "If It Fits Your Macros" (IIFYM) Ka Concept Kya Hai?

Fitness industry me aajkal sabse zada chalne wali term **IIFYM** hai yani (If It Fits Your Macros). Is diet strategy ka logic ye hai ki aap kuch bhi kha sakte ho – chahye thoda meetha ya snaks hon – shart sirf ye hai ki unke andar chupe hue protein, carbs aur fats rozmara aapke *Macro Target* se exceed nahi hone chaiye. Is lchiiley (flexible) rules ki vjhahi se log boring diet pr vi lamby samaye tak tike rejthte hain. Aur ek dum clear target figure ke liye aapko pehle **Macro calculator** ki sahayata se apne numbers janne honge.
