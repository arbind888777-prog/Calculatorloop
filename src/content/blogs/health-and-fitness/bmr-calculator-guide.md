## BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) Kya Hota Hai?

BMR ka full form hai **Basal Metabolic Rate**. Saral aur Seedhe shabdon mein kahe toh, agar aap poore 24 ghante bistar se bahar na nikle, na haath-pair hilaye aur na hi dimag pe koi zor dale, sirf zinda rehna ka kaam karein, tab bhi aapki body internally functions karti hai (jaise ki saans lena, dil ka dhadakna, khoon ban banana, naye cells paida karna, temperature ko regularise karna aur khana pachana). 

In sabhi basic functions, jinhe life-sustaining needs kaha jata hai, unko pura karne ke liye body naturally jo energy/calories consume karti hai—chahe aap araam ki sthiti mein ho—use hi BMR kehte hain. Human body me consume hone wali daily calories mein se almost 60-70% hissa kewal is BMR category mein he jata hai. Sirf 20% humari physical movement aur walking se hoti hai aur bacha 10% khana pachaane ki urja me waste hoti hai jise Thermic Effect of Food kaha jata hai.

Yadi aap apne body ki actual baseline check karna chahte hain toh aap bina kisi error ke health experts dawara pramanit tools jaise **BMR Calculator** ki sahayata se calculation kar sakte hain.

## Metabolism (Chayapchay) aur BMR me Farak

Aksar log Metabolic rate aur Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) me confuse ho jate hain.
Yad rakhyein, metabolism ek bada terms hai jo define karta hai ki poore din apko digest, work workout aur breathing mila ke kitni urja ki zaroorat padhti hai, jab ki BMR kewal us metabolism ka mool foundation point (basic survival need) hai. Fast metabolism ka matlab hi high BMR se hai. 

## BMR Calculation Ka Scientific Formula Kya Hai?

Aap humare smart **BMR calculator tool** ke zariye to turant number pata kar he sakte hain, prantu basic formulae pichle decade me time-to-time upgrade ho chuke hain:

### The Harris-Benedict Equation:
Ise 1919 mein develop kiya gya, jo ki originally logo ke sharir ki height aur age wagairah dekhta tha:
*Puruso (Men) k liye:* BMR = 88.362 + (13.397 x wazan in kg) + (4.799 x height in cm) - (5.677 x Umar(age))
*Mahilaon (Women) k liye:* BMR = 447.593 + (9.247 x wazan in kg) + (3.098 x height in cm) - (4.330 x Umar(age))

### The Mifflin-St Jeor Equation:
Ise kafi had tak modern aur most accurate tareeka mana jata hai. Aajkal adhiktar professional dieticians iska isteemal karte hain:
*Men:* BMR = (10 x weight in kg) + (6.25 x height in cm) - (5 x age in years) + 5
*Women:* BMR = (10 x weight in kg) + (6.25 x height in cm) - (5 x age in years) - 161

## Weight Lose Karne Ya Gain Karne Mein BMR Ka Role

Ab sabse important sawal, aakir yeh fitness field mein kyu pooja jata hai?
Ans: **Calorie Tracking** ke bina fitness incomplete hai. Aur kitni calorie aapko required hai yeh aapka BMR batave hai.
**Yadi BMR ke baraabar khaayenge** to apka weight strictly usi paimane pe fix rahega jisme wih present condition me hai. Usay "Maintenance level" ni kahete (Maintenance hota hai TDEE, jisme physical work involve ho).
**Weight badhane ke lye** (For bulking/gain): Aadmi ko apne BMR aur total physical movements calculate karke unse roughly 300 to 500 extra unit (Surplus) of energy khani padti hai. (TDEE + Surplus)
**Weight ghatane k liye** (For reduction/cutting): Aadmi ko naturally physical working output ko barhana padata hai ya apne intake (required calroies) me se daily 300 to 500 unit reduce karni padhti hain (Calorie Deficit).

**WARNING**: Kisi vi condition mein kabhi apni BMR level (the survival energy point) se neeche calories eat na karein. Ussey starvation condition me jake body muscles ko destroy/break down karna shuruhh kardeti hain jisey apko bimariya, malnutrition (kuposhan), baal jhadna, aor depression hone ki probabilities almost certain level pe pahonch jaati hai. 

## BMR Kin Cheezo Par Depend Karta Hai? (Factors that Influence BMR)

Alag alag insanaon ka BMR kabhi ek jesa bilkul nahi hota. Kya cheezein ise vary karwati han?

1. **Muscle Mass**: Jin logo ka body structure lean muscles ka bana hota hai (Fat percentage kam hoga) unka BMR hamesha ucha rate me rehta hai. Islie athletes khub khane par bhi fit rhte ho aur mote nahi hote. Fat cell ke muqabale Muscle cell apne aakar (size) ko banaye rakhne k lyeah zada calories eat karte hain. 
2. **Age (Umar)**: Umar badhne ke saTh BMR me natural decline aana lazmi hota hai q-ki sharir internally wear and tear ho kar apne max energy level ki bajai slow kam karta hai. (Isiliye badhti umar ke log easily mote dikhai detey hain).
3. **Gender**: Menstration cycle, aur naturally muscle mass build up k karan common taur par females ka basal rate lower point par chalta hai aur Men ka basal level thoda sa upar (approx 5 to 10% higher) hota hai.
4. **Genetics / Hereditary**: Aakhirkaar humesha DNA sabki base body mechanics tay karta hai. Apki genes ki programming agar fast metabolism output dene k liye hwi he, tab aap un lucky members me he hai jinka bmr logically uper rheta he.
5. **Weather (Mausam)**: Sardiyo ke mausam (Cold environments) me body ko garam rakhne ke liye insaan ke andar body jyada shakti burn krti hain. Result: higher BMR.

## How to Increase Your BMR? (BMR badhane ke tarike)

Jab logon ko ye sab samajh aata hai toh unka common sawaal nikal ke ye hi ata hai ki 'Hum apna BMR ya Metabolism kaise badhaen?'. Health enthusiasts aur professionals niman baato ko focus me rakhkr advice karte hain:
*   **Muscles banayen (Lift Weights):** Jaise bataya ki muscle ki quantity jyada rahna se aap automatically daily basis pe resting state me weight ya fat loose karte rahene. Strength exercises zarur kre.
*   **Protein rich diet lein :** Protein ko bachana (digest karna) carb or fat base ki items ke reference men body ke lie bahut zyada energy spend krata he jisko medical terms me TEF (Themic Effect of Food) bola he jiska outcome better metabolic rates hote hai.
*   **Active (khush) Rahiye :** Stress and thakan apne dmag ka function kam krke body reaction slow krdiya jis wja se direct metabolism decline hone lag jana mana jata hi. 
*   **Hydration par dhiyan rakhiye :** Paani bahut acchah internal cooler/solvent ka kam karna. Proper hydrated rhne waale aadmi ka digestive system naturally clear rahne ke krn organ level par performance highly retain rahhti.

**Q.1 BMR aur BMI men kya farak hai?**
Ans: BMI aapki height aur weight ke aapas me equation par aadharit hai jo aapka motapa ya dubla-pan napta hai, jabki BMR apke internally organs ya function run krne ke lie min-requirement wale calorie numbers btata hai.
**Q.2 Agar mera BMR score 1500 aaye, to main pure diman mei sirf 1500 calories loon?**
Ans: Bilkul nahin! Aap pure din me bed to nahi padde rahiye, kuch chalenge bhi, aur thoda dmag bhi chalaynge to wahn jo work hai (TDEE) vo apko 1500 ki bjya maybe 1900 unit tak ka jarurat paida lkr de. To diet set hamesha BMR par na ho kar apne TDEE factor k lie karna he.
