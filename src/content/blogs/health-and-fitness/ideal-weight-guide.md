## Ideal Weight (Aadarsh Vajan) Aakhir Hota Kya Hai?

Ideal Weight us wazan ko darshata hai jiske as-pass rakhne se insaan ke bimariyon ka shikar hone (jaise diabetes, high blood pressure aur heart issue) ka risk sabse minimal (kam-tareen) ho jata hai. Medically, ye ek disease-free point hota hai. Ise height-weight ka best combination bhi kaha ja sakta hai. 

Misaal ke taur par, ek 6 foot ke aadmi ka wazan agar 55 kg ho to wo malnutrition (kamzori) ka shikar lagega aur wahin agar wo 100 kg ka ho to wo charbi se ghira hua feel karega. Dono hi situations mein health compromised hai. Ideal wazan in dono extreme limits ke beech ka "Healthy Spot" (lagbhag 75kg – 85kg) darshata hai.

## Ideal Weight Nikaalne Ke Siddhant / Formulae

Hamara **Ideal Weight Calculator** aasmaan se numbers nhi laata, iske peeche well-established western aur scientific formulas chupe ha jo globally recognize kiye jate hain. Yaha hum kuch famous method batate hain jo hamari system algorithm me background calculation karte hien:

### 1. J. Devine Formula (1974)
Medical dozings aur treatments (jaise chemotherapy injection dosage set krne) ke liye sabsa zyada maana jata he.
*   **Purush (Men):** 50.0 kg + 2.3 kg per inch 5 feet se aage (over 5 feet)
*   **Mahilayen (Women):** 45.5 kg + 2.3 kg per inch (over 5 feet)

### 2. J. D. Robinson Formula (1983)
Ye Devine formulae mein he thoda sa update that jo thodi broad ranges data tha:
*   **Men:** 52 kg + 1.9 kg per inch 5 feet pvr bdn pr.
*   **Women:** 49 kg + 1.7 kg per inch aagey bdenr pe.

### 3. D. R. Miller Formula (1983)
Ye calculation basically standard western lifestyle aur diet ko dekthate huve design kya gaya tha, jo lean-mass index pe focus karta he.
*   **Men:** 56.2 kg + 1.41 kg per inch over 5 feet
*   **Women:** 53.1 kg + 1.36 kg per inch over 5 feet
