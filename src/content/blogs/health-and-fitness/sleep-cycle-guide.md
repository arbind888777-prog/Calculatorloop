## Sleep Cycle (Neend Ka Chakra) Aakhir Kya Hai?

Jab hum raat ko sote hain, tab humara dimaag (brain) aur sharir (body) puri raat ek fixed aaram ki state me nahi rehta, balki woh alag-alag phases (charano) se guzarta hai. In phases ko mul-roop se do categories me baanta jata hai: **NREM (Non-Rapid Eye Movement)** aur **REM (Rapid Eye Movement)**.

1. **NREM Stage 1 & 2 (Halki Neend):** Yeh sone ki shuruaat hoti hai. Ise Light Sleep kehte hain jaha se aahat aane par aap aasani se uth sakte hain.
2. **NREM Stage 3 (Gehri Neend):** Ise Deep Sleep kehte hain. Yaha par aakar body apne tissues ko repair karti hai, immunity badhati hai aur nayi urja ka nirmaan karti hai. Is avastha jme aadmi ko uthana sabse mushkil hota hai.
3. **REM Sleep (Sapne dekhne ki stage):** Yeh aakhiri stage hoti hai. Is phase mein dimaag active ho jata hai, aapko sapne aate hain aur memory (yaad-dasht) store hoti hai. 

In teeno stages ko mila kar ek "Sleep Cycle" banti hai. Aam taur par ek cycle ko pura hone mein **90 Minutes (Dedh ghanta)** lagta hai. Poori raat mein jab sote hue ek swaasthya insaan apni 4 se 6 cycles puri karta hai, tab subh fresh udhta hia .

## Neend se Uthne Ka Sahi Waqt (The 90-Minute Rule)

Science kehti hai ki jab koi sleep cycle chal rahi hoti hai (vishesh roop se Deep sleep ya REM phase) aur apka alarm achanak apko utha de, tab aap din bhar "Sleep Inertia" (neend ka nasha / susti) mehsus karenge. 

Lekin agar aapka alarm thik us waqt baje jab aapki ek **90 minute ki sleep cycle poori ho chuki ho** (yaani jab agla chakra shuru hone wala ho, jahan neend naturally sabse halki hoti hai), toh aap instantly taaza aur urjawan (energetic) uthatay hain.
