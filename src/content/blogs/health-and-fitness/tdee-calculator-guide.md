# TDEE Calculator Guide: Apni Total Daily Energy Expenditure Ko Samjhein

Agar aap fitness ki duniya me naye hain ya fir lambe samay se gym ja rahe hain, tab bhi 'Calories In vs Calories Out' ka rule sabse universal aur atal niyam maana jata hai. Log diet control karte hain, ghanton cardio karte hain, par fir bhi unka wazan ya to wahi ruka rehta hai ya kamzor hone lagta hai, kyunki wo apne **TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure)** number ko ignore kar dete hain. 

TDEE ka ek simple sa meaning hai ki aapka sharir ek din (24 ghante) mein total kitni energy (calories) burn karta hai apni activities or life sustain functions ko maintain karne ke liye. Bina is number ko jane, weight gain ya loss karna andhere mein teer chalane jaisa hota hai. Yahi wajah hai ki aaj modern fitness industry me **TDEE Calculator** ki itni badhi demand aa chuki hai. ## TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) Kya Hai?

Aam insaan ki zindagi kevel bistar par letna nahi hota, uski zindagi mein walk (chalna), seedhi chadna, workout/gym karna, dimagi kaam (office work/studies) sub shamil hota hai. BMR bataata hai ki sirf saanse chalane or zinda rahne ko kitni body energy chahiye, jab hi **TDEE** waha ginti deta hai jisme dinbhar ka body movement bhi shaamil ho.

Kaha jaye toh TDEE ek complete daily energy report formlula he = (BMR + TEF + NEAT + EAT)

Aayiye dekhte hain TDEE calculator kin cheezon ka total/sum hota hai:
1.  **BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate):** Total khapne wali daily calories ka approx **60-70%**. Yahi sabhi internal aango tak khun pahunchane aur heart work krwane wali urja banti he.
2.  **NEAT (Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis):** Total energy ka **15-20%**. Ye pure un physical movments ka number hota he (jaise keval paer hilana, safai karna, typing karma, brush wagera) ko ke proper strict excercise zone se alg ke movements.
3.  **TEF (Thermic Effect of Food):** Total consumed energy ka roughly **10%**. Apne chana ya dhoodh khaya par uska protein complex todana aasan nahi – shareer pachaane k lie naturally digestion ko internally speed-up krtay hai, and yahi digestion calorie consumption lagwata hhai.
4.  **EAT (Exercise Activity Thermogenesis):** Aapke pure TDEE me bacha aakhiri **5-10%** hissa. Jis workout ya run par aap focus dalte the (Treadmill par 30 minutes, wagera) wo siraf chota element banke aata he.

## TDEE Calc Ki Jaankari Hona Kyu Itee Jarrui Hai?

Yadi aap gadi se travel plan kar ragein hain, toh journey ke route /petrol tank consumption pattern ki math pehle lgate hi - thek isiy trh weight loss karna ya healthy lifestyle bnai rhkhna ke peechy exact caloric consumption numbers (yaani TDEE) k zaroorat parti hn! 

-   **Weight Maintainenance:** Apna same weight as is chhodne ke lie logoen ko TDEE ke bilkul equal khaana prega. Yadi apka TDEE calculation batata hai 2200 unit aur agar utna hi diet kha rhe, toh koi vjn fluctuate nehi krega!
-   **Fat loss and Cutting:** Fitness trainers, experts suggest apko TDEE ki daily energy ki bjai daily diet input ko roughly 300 to 500 reduce karein jise **Calorie Deficit** nam kehte hia. Total weight automatically kam padhega
-   **Weight gain and Bulking:** Yahan aap required total energy k opposite ja ree hote hai jahan muscles mass body grow karnay lagta hia. Use the **Calorie Surplus**. Approx daily 300 t0 500 TDEE unit uupar se liya kre.

## Activity Multiplier (Kriya Gunak)

TDEE nikale ka formula asaan he — Aapke pehle aayye the BMR number ke sath ek fixed physical physical "Activity Multiplier" ko gunaa (multiply) kiya gae hain:
*   **Sedentary Lifestyle (Baithe hue kām karna)**: Office ki desk jobs. Bahut kam chalna-firna. (BMR * 1.2)
*   **Lightly Active (Thoda bhut kriya-sheel)**: Hafte me 1-3 din simple walk / halki exercises. (BMR * 1.375)
*   **Moderately Active (Madhyam kriya-sheel)**: Hafte me 3-5 times sports ya moderate training gym. (BMR * 1.55)
*   **Very Active (Acchi trha s sckriye)**: 6 to 7 day of high intense gymming, athletes ya daily basis ki bahut mehnat. (BMR * 1.725)
*   **Extra Active (Bahut Jyada):** Professional athletes ya mazddori, marathon training. (BMR * 1.9)
