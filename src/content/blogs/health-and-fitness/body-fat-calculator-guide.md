## Body Fat Percentage Kya Hota Hai?

Body Fat Percentage (BFP) aapke sharir mein kul (total) fat ka anupaat hai jise aapke pure vajan se divide karke percentage me dikhaya jata hai. Yaani aapki body mass ka kitna hissa keval charbi (Fat Tissue) se bana hai.

Sharir me maujut sabkuch fat nahi hota. Fat ke alawa kya hai:
* **Haddiyan (Bones)**
* **Paani (Body Water)**
* **Androoni Ang (Internal Organs)**
* **Manspeshiyan (Muscles)**

In sabhi chizon ke wazan ko fat se alag rakha jata hai, jise hum (Lean Body Mass) kehte hain. Agar aapka Body Fat percentage 20% aata hai iska matlab bacha hua 80% lamba-chaura hissa muscle, water ya bones se bana hai, jo ki healthy hai!

## Ek Healthy Body Fat Range Kitni Honi Chahiye?

Yeh range kabhi gender-neutral (same for men/women) nahi hoti. Bacho ko paida karne aur hormones balance maintain karne ke kuddarti functions ki wajah sey auraton ko hamesha thoda higher proportion of body fat chahiye hota hai adhiyon ke numbers kay reference my.

Aaiye *American Council on Exercise (ACE)* ke standards dekte hain:

### Mardo Ke Liye (Categories For Men)
* **Essential Fat (Jeene ke liye niyatm nnyuntm zaroorat):** 2% – 5% (Sirf professional bodybuilder competition ke time yaha hote).
* **Athletes (Sportsman shape):** 6% – 13% 
* **Fitness (Healthy & fit body):** 14% – 17%
* **Acceptable (Samaanya):** 18% – 24%
* **Obese (Motapa, Khatre ka ishara):** 25% ya usse zyaada.

### Mahilao Ke Liye (Categories For Women)
* **Essential Fat:** 10% – 13% 
* **Athletes:** 14% – 20%
* **Fitness:** 21% – 24%
* **Acceptable:** 25% – 31%
* **Obese (Motapa):** 32% ya usse zyaada.
